I work as a Senior Scientist at [Marathon Fusion](https://www.marathonfusion.com). 

From January 2023 through July 2025 I was a staff research physicist at the Princeton Plasma Physics Laboratory. 

Before that I was a postdoc at Princeton University, working with the [Plasma Control Group](https://control.princeton.edu/) to develop an open-source "systems code" for tokamaks, and with [ZERO Lab](https://zero.lab.princeton.edu/) to study fusion's value as part of future electricity markets.

I completed my PhD in Princeton's Program in Plasma Physics in 2020. My thesis work was on developing a piece of technology with application for nuclear fusion reactors.
Later, in 2023, I shared the [Kaul Foundation Prize](https://www.pppl.gov/news/2023/pppl-researchers-earn-kaul-foundation-prize-fusion-cooling-system) for this work.
